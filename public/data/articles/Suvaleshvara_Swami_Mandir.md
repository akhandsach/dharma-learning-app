**Location:** ~15kms from Tiruvallur on the way to Tiruttani (NH205).

**Timings:** 6:30am-12:00pm and 4:00pm-8:00pm
thumb

**K**ṣ**hetra Purāṇam:** A 500 year old temple contructed by Chola kings. The main deity is Lord Shiva with the name *Suvaleśvara* (One who controls māya) and consort as shri *Kāmākṣhi* devi.

**Other Main Deities:** Kubereshvara, Dakshina murty, Kalabhairava, Vinayaka, Anjaneya, Subrahmanya, Navagrahas, Kashi Vishveshvara.

**Near by Temples:** Tiruvallūr (~15kms), Tirupp*ā*ccūr (Vasīśvara swami temple at distance ~15km), Tiruttaṇi (~20kms).

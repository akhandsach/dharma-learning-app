DharmaWiki serves as a common comprehensive reference platform for both the novice and the scholars and actively promotes the following 
#Initiation of resurgence of ancient time-tested knowledge of dharmika jivana vidhana to promote holistic approach towards life in the generations to come.
#Language resource creation (wikisource repository) its development and population with the knowledge base from ancient Sanskrit Vedic sources.
#Aim to capture, complement, supplement and disseminate knowledge of India's integral practices.
#Bringing to light the unbiased dharmika perspectives on social, economic, political, and cultural aspects presented in Vedic literature.
#Providing a technological workspace to bring forward the views of traditional scholars (mainstream or otherwise) and their scholarly works that have been unexplored.
#Developing narratives grounded in dharmika traditions for mainstream subjects such as medicine, sciences, economics, judiciary, chemistry, geology, ecology, astronomy, mathematics and vocational subjects.
#Collaboration with national and international institutes to obtain recorded perspectives based on Purushartha Drshti. 
#Strive to dispel myths created by western indoctrination, presenting the concepts to youth with logic and reasoning in a lucid language. 
#Building crisp yet comprehensive articles free from interpretations, psychoanalysis, extrapolations, preachings, and personal impressions guided by Dharmawiki policies.

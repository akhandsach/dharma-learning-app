Shakhas can be classified into two types
# compilations without any variations in mantras from the original structure of vedas
# compilations with some variations in mantras different from original structure of vedas
According to MahaBhasya of Patanjali, there were 1131 Shakhas (21+101+1000+9) prevalent during his times. <blockquote>एकविंशतिधा बाह्वृच्यम्। एकशतमध्वर्युशाखाः। सहस्रवर्त्मा सामवेदः। नवधाथर्वणो वेदः। पातन्जल महाभाष्य, पस्प-शाह्निक। </blockquote><blockquote>*ekaviṁśatidhā bāhvr̥cyam। ekaśatamadhvaryuśākhāḥ। sahasravartmā sāmavedaḥ। navadhātharvaṇo vedaḥ। pātanjala mahābhāṣya, paspa-śāhnika।* </blockquote>This includes the 4 Veda Samhitas. However, now only 16 of these Shakhas are available, which are as follows:

1.     5 of ऋग्वेदः **शाकल: (Shaakala)** (with and without Vala Khilya Sukta), बाष्कल: (Bashkala), आश्वलायन: (Ashvalayana), शाङ्खायन: (Shankhayana) and माण्डूकायन (Mandukaayana)

2.     6 of यजुर्वेदः

a.      3 of शुक्ल-यजुर्वेदः**वाजसनेयी (Vaajasaneyi)** or माध्यन्दिनी (Maadhyandini) and काण्व (Kaanva)।

b.      3 of कृष्ण-यजुर्वेदः तैत्तिरीय: (Taittiriya), मैत्रायणी (Maitraayani), कठ: (Katha), and कपिष्ठल (Kapishhthala)।

3.     3 of सामवेदः **कौथुमीय (Kauthumiya)**, राणायनीय (Ranaayaniya) and जैमिनीय (Jaiminiya)

4.     2 of अथर्ववेदः **शौनक (Shaunak)** and पैप्लाद: (Paipalaada)

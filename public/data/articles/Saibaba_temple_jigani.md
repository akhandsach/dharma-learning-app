## Temple Brief Description
Saibaba Mandir is a divine religious place located in Jigani Anekal Main Road. Its also called as Dakshina Shirdi Sai Baba Mandir. The Temple is inside a gated community villa project called Prashanti Grand Villa which you can see on main road itself. It is about 7 Km from Jigani APC Circle towards Anekal main road. It is located in a serene place.
### Estimated age of temple
Saibaba Mandir was inaugurated in June 2019 and it is one of the good temples in that location.
